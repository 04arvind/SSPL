Physical Efficiency Test (PET)

Candidate TypeRequirement
Male Lift 35 kg for 100 meters in 2 minutes without putting it down.
Run 1000 meters in 4 minutes 15 seconds.
Female Lift 20 kg for 100 meters in 2 minutes without putting it down.
Run 1000 meters in 5 minutes 40 seconds.
PWD Exempted from PET, must pass medical examination.

Document Verification & Empanelment

  • Produce original documents and self-attested copies.
  • Verify: education, category, Aadhaar/PAN, etc.
  • Medical fitness test after document check.
  • Final selection based on merit and medical clearance.
  • Invalid documents or medical failure = disqualification.
