Mensuration

1. What is Mensuration?

Mensuration deals with the measurement of geometric figures, including area, perimeter, and volume of 2D and 3D shapes.

2. 2D Shape Formulas

  • Rectangle: Area = length × breadth; Perimeter = 2(length + breadth).
  • Square: Area = side²; Perimeter = 4 × side.
  • Triangle: Area = (1/2) × base × height; Perimeter = sum of sides.
  • Circle: Area = πr²; Circumference = 2πr.

3. Example 1: Rectangle

Find the area and perimeter of a rectangle with length 10 cm and breadth 5 cm.

  • Area = 10 × 5 = 50 cm².
  • Perimeter = 2(10 + 5) = 30 cm.
  • Answer: Area = 50 cm², Perimeter = 30 cm.

4. Example 2: Circle

Find the area and circumference of a circle with radius 7 cm (use π = 22/7).

  • Area = (22/7) × 7² = 154 cm².
  • Circumference = 2 × (22/7) × 7 = 44 cm.
  • Answer: Area = 154 cm², Circumference = 44 cm.

5. 3D Shape Formulas


  • Cube: Volume = side³; Surface area = 6 × side².
  • Cuboid: Volume = length × breadth × height; Surface area = 2(lb + bh + lh).
  • Cylinder: Volume = πr²h; Curved surface area = 2πrh.
  • Sphere: Volume = (4/3)πr³; Surface area = 4πr².

6. Example 3: Cuboid

Find the volume and surface area of a cuboid with dimensions 6 cm, 4 cm, 3 cm.

  • Volume = 6 × 4 × 3 = 72 cm³.
  • Surface area = 2(6×4 + 4×3 + 6×3) = 108 cm².
  • Answer: Volume = 72 cm³, Surface area = 108 cm².

7. Practice Problems

  1. Find the area of a square with side 8 cm.
  2. Find the circumference of a circle with radius 14 cm (π = 22/7).
  3. Find the volume of a cube with side 5 cm.
  4. Find the area of a triangle with base 10 cm and height 6 cm.
  5. Find the curved surface area of a cylinder with radius 7 cm and height 10 cm (π = 22/7).

8. Solutions

  1. Area = 8² = 64 cm².
  2. Circumference = 2 × (22/7) × 14 = 88 cm.
  3. Volume = 5³ = 125 cm³.
  4. Area = (1/2) × 10 × 6 = 30 cm².
  5. Curved surface area = 2 × (22/7) × 7 × 10 = 440 cm².

9. Tips

  • Memorize formulas for common shapes.
  • Check units (e.g., cm² for area, cm³ for volume).
  • Practice problems with mixed shapes.
